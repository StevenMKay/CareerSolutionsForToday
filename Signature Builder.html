Install Your Signature

First, click "Copy Signature" above, then follow the steps for your email client.

1
Open Gmail and click the gear icon (top right), then "See all settings".
2
In the "General" tab, scroll down to "Signature".
3
Click "+ Create new", name your signature, then click inside the text box.
4
Paste your copied signature (Ctrl+V / Cmd+V). The formatting and images will transfer.
5
Under "Signature defaults", select your new signature for New emails and Replies/Forwards.
6
Scroll to the bottom and click "Save Changes".
1
Open Outlook Desktop. Go to File > Options > Mail > Signatures.
2
Click "New" and give your signature a name.
3
Paste your copied signature into the editor box (Ctrl+V).
4
Set it as the default signature for New Messages and Replies/Forwards.
5
Click "OK" to save.
1
Open Apple Mail. Go to Mail > Settings > Signatures.
2
Select your email account and click the "+" button to add a new signature.
3
Paste your copied signature into the right-hand preview area (Cmd+V).
4
Uncheck "Always match my default message font" to preserve formatting.
5
Set it as the default signature using the "Choose Signature" dropdown.
1
Go to outlook.com or outlook.office.com and sign in.
2
Click the gear icon (top right), then "View all Outlook settings".
3
Go to Mail > Compose and reply.
4
Click "+ New signature", name it, then paste your signature into the editor.
5
Set it as the default for New messages and Replies/Forwards, then click "Save".
