Site Features & Help
How do I change a team member's access level?
Go to Team Settings in the sidebar. Find the member in the list, click the role dropdown next to their name, and select Admin or Member. Changes take effect on their next page load.
How can I keep a project confidential or separate from others?
Open the project's detail modal and check the Confidential checkbox. Confidential projects are visible only to Admins and the project's owner. Members see a blurred card with no details.
How do I add team members to my organization?
Navigate to Team Settings in the sidebar. Use the Invite Team Member form to enter their email address. They'll receive an invitation link to join your workspace.
How do I create custom fields for project entries?
Open Admin Settings in the sidebar, then navigate to the Form Builder tab. Add custom fields (text, number, date, dropdown, etc.) by selecting a type and label, then click Add Field. Drag to reorder.
How do I generate a PowerPoint presentation from my projects?
Click the Slides button in the top toolbar. Choose the projects you want to include, select a theme, and click Generate. A branded .pptx file will download to your computer automatically.
How does the AI analysis feature work?
Click the AI button in the toolbar. The AI reads your current project data and can generate executive summaries, identify risks, recommend next steps, and flag overdue or stalled projects. Configure the AI model in Admin → AI Settings.
Can I assign projects to specific team members?
Yes. When creating or editing a project, use the Assigned To dropdown, which is automatically populated from your org chart and team members list. You can also set up automatic notifications when assignments change in the Notifications & Workflows menu.
How do I set up email notifications for project events?
Open Notifications & Workflows in the sidebar. Go to the Workflows tab (Admins only) and add a rule: choose a trigger event (created, assigned, status changed, completed), select who to notify, and optionally require approval. Notifications go to each recipient's inbox.
How do I archive or close out a completed project?
Open the project's detail modal, change the Status to Completed or Closed (depending on your configured status labels), and save. Completed projects can be filtered out of your main view using the status filter at the top of the dashboard.
How do I use the org chart to organize team structure?
Go to Team Settings and open the Org Chart tab. Add positions, link them to team members, and set reporting relationships. The Assigned To dropdown on every project will reflect team members from your org chart automatically.
