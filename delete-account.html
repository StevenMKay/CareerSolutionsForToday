🗑️ Delete Your Account & Data

Career Solutions for Today — Resume Portfolio / Career Resume Builder · Last Updated: April 2026

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to submit a deletion request, what will be removed, and what (if anything) we are legally required to retain.

App & Developer

  • App name: Career Resume Builder (also referred to as "Resume Portfolio")
  • Developer: Career Solutions for Today
  • Contact email: stevenk@careersolutionsfortoday.com

Option 1 — Delete From Inside the App (Fastest)

If you can still sign in, you can delete your account and data yourself in under a minute:

  1. Open the Career Resume Builder app (or visit careersolutionsfortoday.com/resumebuilder.html) and sign in.
  2. Tap your profile avatar in the top-right corner to open the account menu.
  3. Select "Delete Account & Data".
  4. Confirm the deletion in the pop-up. Your account, resumes, career plans, uploaded photos, and saved settings are removed immediately.
Note: If you have an active paid subscription, you must cancel it first (the app will prompt you and link to "Manage Subscription"). This prevents future charges after deletion.

Option 2 — Request Deletion by Email

If you no longer have access to the app, have uninstalled it, or prefer we handle it for you, send us an email:

  • To: stevenk@careersolutionsfortoday.com
  • Subject: Delete My Account and Data
  • Include in the body:
    • The email address associated with your account
    • A brief confirmation: "I request permanent deletion of my account and data."

Please send the request from the email address on the account so we can verify ownership. We will confirm the deletion and complete it within 30 days of receiving a valid request (most requests are processed within 72 hours).

What Gets Deleted

When your request is processed, the following is permanently removed from our active systems:

  • Your Firebase Authentication record (email, Google sign-in link, password hash)
  • Your profile (name, contact info, preferences)
  • All saved resumes, 90-day plans, and job-application tracking data
  • Uploaded files, including profile photos and parsed resume documents
  • Usage records (free-tier counters, AI-usage counters, analysis cache)
  • Any published share links associated with your resumes

What May Be Retained (and Why)

DataRetention periodReason
Stripe billing and tax records (transaction IDs, invoice amounts) Up to 7 years Required by U.S. tax and financial-recordkeeping law. Held by Stripe, not in our app database.
Encrypted backups Up to 90 days Standard operational backups; overwritten on a rolling schedule. Not used for any other purpose.
Anonymized / aggregated analytics Indefinite Cannot be linked back to you; used only for product improvement.
Records required by law (e.g., fraud investigations, legal holds) As required Only if specifically compelled by law.
