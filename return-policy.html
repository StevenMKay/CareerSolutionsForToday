Return, Refund & Cancellation Policy

Because Resume Portfolio Builder is a digital service, there is no physical item to return. This policy explains free trial access, lifetime-access refunds, cancellation, and how to contact us for billing support.

📋 Policy Overview

Thank you for using Career Solutions for Today. Resume Portfolio Builder is a digital service delivered online. Because there is no physical product to return, this policy explains how free trial access, lifetime-access refunds, cancellation, and billing support are handled.

🔒 Digital Service

Resume Portfolio Builder is a digital service. There is no physical item to ship or return. Access is provided online.

🆓 7-Day Free Trial

Resume Portfolio Builder may offer a 7-day free trial so users can explore the service before purchasing lifetime access. No payment is charged during the free trial.

If you decide the service is not right for you during the free trial, you can cancel or stop using the service before purchasing. Because no payment is charged during the free trial, there is no refund needed for trial cancellation.

✨ Try Before You Buy

Try the builder before purchasing. If you do not purchase lifetime access, there is no charge.

💳 $99 Lifetime Access Purchase

The $99 lifetime access purchase is charged immediately when you complete the purchase. Because this is a digital service, access is provided online after purchase.

If you purchase lifetime access and decide it is not the right fit, you may request a refund within 7 days of purchase by emailing stevenk@careersolutionsfortoday.com.

After 7 days, lifetime access purchases are generally non-refundable because access to the digital service has already been provided.

🚪 Cancellation

If you are using the free trial, you can cancel or stop using the service before purchasing lifetime access. No payment is charged during the free trial.

If you purchased $99 lifetime access, there is no recurring subscription to cancel. However, you may request a refund within 7 days of purchase by emailing stevenk@careersolutionsfortoday.com.

If any future recurring subscription plan is offered, cancellation will stop future billing. Charges already processed may not be refundable unless required by law or approved by Career Solutions for Today.

✅ Refunds May Be Approved For

  • $99 lifetime access refund requests made within 7 days of purchase
  • Duplicate charges or billing errors
  • Technical issues that prevent reasonable access to the service and cannot be resolved
  • Purchases that were not activated or delivered as expected
  • Other situations reviewed and approved by Career Solutions for Today

❌ Refunds Are Generally Not Provided For

  • Requests made after the 7-day refund window for lifetime access
  • Lack of use after access was provided
  • Change of mind after the refund period has passed
  • Issues caused by unsupported devices, browsers, or user-side technical limitations
  • Requests unrelated to a billing issue, access issue, or eligible refund window

📞 How to Request a Refund or Billing Help

To request a refund review, cancellation help, or billing support, email stevenk@careersolutionsfortoday.com.

Please include:

  • Your name
  • Email address used for purchase
  • Purchase date
  • Plan purchased
  • Reason for the request
  • Any relevant screenshots or error messages

We aim to respond within 1–2 business days. If a refund is approved, it will be issued to the original payment method. Processing times may vary depending on the payment provider and your financial institution.

Need Help With a Purchase?

For refund requests, trial questions, or billing support, email us and include the email address used for your purchase.
