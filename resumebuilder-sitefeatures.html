1Import & Target

Review your role-match score and targeted recommendations.

  • Match-score guidance across keywords, role fit, impact, and clarity

Frequently asked

Does uploading my resume make it public?

No. Uploading and editing your resume happens inside the builder. A public page is only created when you choose to publish, and you decide what content to include.

Do I need an account to try the builder?

Yes. Signing in lets the builder save your imported resume, target roles, and any drafts so you can come back later. You can sign in with Google or with email and password from the login page.

What file types can I upload?

You can upload a PDF or DOCX of your existing resume. The builder extracts experience, skills, and education from the document so you don't have to re-type everything.

How does the role-match score work?

It's a guidance score across keywords, role fit, impact, and clarity. It points out areas that may need more evidence and surfaces copy-ready rewrite suggestions, but you stay in control of which edits to apply.

What does “publishing” actually do?

It generates a responsive public web page for the version you choose. You decide what's included before publishing, and you can republish to update the page later. See a live published example.

Can I still download a regular resume document?

Yes. You can export a print-ready PDF or editable Word document using one of several ATS-friendly resume styles, with color options for personalization.

Is this for first-time job seekers, career changers, or experienced professionals?

All of the above. The flow is the same: import what you have, target the role you want, refine, and either share online or download to apply.
