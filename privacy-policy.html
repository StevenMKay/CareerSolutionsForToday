🔐 Privacy Policy

Career Solutions for Today — Resume Portfolio · Version 1.0 · Last Updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when using Resume Portfolio, including:

  • Name, email address, and account details
  • Resume content, work history, and career-related data
  • Uploaded files (e.g., resumes, documents, images)
  • 90-day plans and other generated content
  • Payment-related metadata (processed securely via third parties)

We also automatically collect:

  • Device and browser information
  • IP address
  • Usage data (pages visited, actions taken)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and operate Resume Portfolio
  • Save and manage your resumes and career plans
  • Generate AI-powered suggestions and improvements
  • Process subscriptions and manage billing
  • Improve platform performance and user experience
  • Communicate important updates or support responses

3. AI Processing (OpenAI)

We use OpenAI API services to process certain resume content. We do not use customer content to train our models by default through the API, but data may be retained temporarily by service providers for abuse prevention, security, legal compliance, or operational reasons.

You should avoid entering highly sensitive personal information (Social Security numbers, financial details, etc.).

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using secure cloud services, including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firestore (database)
  • Firebase Storage

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your information, but no system is 100% secure.

5. Camera & Device Permissions

The Career Solutions mobile app may request access to your device camera and photo library solely to allow you to add a profile photo to your resume. This data is not shared with third parties and is only used within the app for the purpose you initiate.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active, as needed to provide services, and as required for legal, tax, or billing obligations. Deleted content may remain in backups for a limited period.

7. Account & Data Deletion

You may delete your account and all associated data — including saved resumes, career plans, profile information, and uploaded files — directly from the account menu using the "Delete Account & Data" option. Account deletion requires that any active subscription be canceled first. Deleted data is permanently removed from our active systems; however, residual copies may persist in backups for a limited period.

8. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access your data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data (see Section 7)
  • Request a copy of your data

To make a request, contact: 📧 stevenk@careersolutionsfortoday.com

9. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies or similar technologies to maintain sessions, improve functionality, and analyze usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

10. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers, including:

  • Stripe — payment processing (Stripe Privacy)
  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, storage (Firebase Privacy)
  • OpenAI — AI processing (OpenAI Privacy)

11. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with service providers needed to operate the platform, when required by law, or to protect rights and prevent fraud.
