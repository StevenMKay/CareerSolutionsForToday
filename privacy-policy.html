🔐 Privacy Policy

Career Solutions for Today — Resume Portfolio & Career Solutions Web / Android App · Version 1.1 · Last Updated: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Career Solutions for Today ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, retains, and deletes your personal information when you use our website (careersolutionsfortoday.com), our Resume Portfolio Android application, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

Google User Data Disclosure

This section is provided to comply with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements, and the Google APIs Terms of Service.

Data Accessed. When you sign in with Google, our Services access the following Google user data via Google Sign-In / Firebase Authentication:

  • Your Google account email address (used as your account identifier)
  • Your display name and profile photo URL (used only if you choose to show them in your account menu or on a generated resume)
  • A Google-issued stable user ID (UID) that links your session to your saved data

We do not request, access, read, download, or copy any other Google user data. We do not access Gmail, Google Drive, Google Contacts, Google Calendar, YouTube private data, or any other Google API scopes beyond basic profile sign-in.

Data Usage. The Google user data we access is used solely to:

  • Authenticate you and keep you signed in across sessions
  • Create and maintain your account record
  • Associate resumes, career plans, saved work, subscriptions, and preferences with your account
  • Send transactional and support email you explicitly request
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and detect abuse or fraud

We do not use Google user data for advertising, profile building, training AI models, or any purpose unrelated to providing the user-facing features you sign up for. We do not sell Google user data.

Data Sharing. We do not sell or rent Google user data. We share Google user data only with the following categories of service providers, strictly to operate the Services you requested:

  • Google / Firebase (Firebase Authentication, Firestore Database, Firebase Storage, Firebase Hosting) — authentication, data storage, and delivery. Governed by the Firebase Privacy & Security policy.
  • Stripe — subscription and payment processing. Receives only the account email and subscription metadata required to process your payment. Governed by the Stripe Privacy Policy.
  • Resend — transactional email delivery (e.g., receipts, password-reset style confirmations). Receives only your email address and the message content. Governed by the Resend Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI — AI processing of resume content you choose to submit for analysis or optimization. OpenAI does not receive your Google account email, profile photo, or UID; it receives only the resume text you explicitly submit. Governed by the OpenAI Privacy Policy.

We may also disclose data when required by law, valid legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users, ourselves, or others. In a corporate transaction (merger, acquisition, asset sale) user data may transfer subject to a successor entity honoring this Privacy Policy.

Data Storage & Protection. Google user data and account-linked content are stored in Google Cloud / Firebase services operated by Google LLC. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS / TLS) and at rest (Google-managed encryption). Access is limited to a small number of authorized operators using Google account-based access controls, two-factor authentication, and least-privilege rules. Firestore security rules restrict every document to its owning user's UID. We do not store passwords ourselves; Google handles password authentication.

Data Retention & Deletion. We retain Google user data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time:

  • From inside the app: open the user menu and choose "Delete Account & Data".
  • From the public web: visit careersolutionsfortoday.com/delete-account.html and follow the instructions.
  • By email request: send a deletion request from the Google account email you signed in with to stevenk@careersolutionsfortoday.com. We will confirm and complete deletion within 30 days.

Deletion removes your account record, resumes, plans, uploaded files, and subscription metadata from our active systems. Residual copies may persist in encrypted backups for up to 30 days before being purged. Records required to be retained for legal, tax, or billing compliance (e.g., payment records) are retained only for the minimum period required by law.

We use OpenAI API services to process certain resume content. We do not use customer content to train our models by default through the API, but data may be retained temporarily by service providers for abuse prevention, security, legal compliance, or operational reasons.

You should avoid entering highly sensitive personal information (Social Security numbers, financial details, etc.).

Your data is stored using secure cloud services, including:

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active, as needed to provide services, and as required for legal, tax, or billing obligations. Deleted content may remain in backups for a limited period.

7. Account & Data Deletion

If you no longer have access to the app, you can also request deletion via our public page: Delete Account & Data.
