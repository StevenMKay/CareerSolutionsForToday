How it works

Three steps. No friction.

01

Start with your story

Import from LinkedIn, upload an old resume, or start from a guided template. We prefill the structure so you just fill in the truth.

02

Tailor to the job

03

Download a pixel-perfect PDF or share a public link. Keep a version per role — all stored safely, synced across web and Android.

Questions

Before you start.

Is Career Resume Builder really free? +

Yes. The free Starter plan has no time limit and no credit card required. You only upgrade if you want AI features, unlimited versions, and premium templates.

Will my resume actually pass ATS systems? +

Every template is hand-tuned to parse cleanly in Workday, Greenhouse, Lever, and Taleo — the four systems used by most Fortune 500 companies. We don't use hidden tables, sidebar columns, or icon-only bullets.

Do I need to download the app, or can I use the web? +

Either works. The web app and Android app share the same account — build on your laptop, tweak on your phone before the interview. iOS is coming soon.
